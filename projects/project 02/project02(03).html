Salad with croutons "Royal":

Guests will be pleased. And most importantly, this salad is very quick to make. The recipe will be very useful as a salad for a birthday. And in the New Year's menu for the New Year 2025.


Ingredients (for 4 servings)
Crab sticks - 240 g
Hard cheese - 300 g
Chicken eggs - 4 pcs.
Croutons - 100 g
Garlic - 2-3 cloves
Lemon (juice) - 0.5 pcs.
Mayonnaise - to taste
