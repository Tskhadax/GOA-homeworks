Warm salad with chicken, sweet peppers and tomatoes:

Meat and vegetables are a great combination for a full meal. Juicy tomatoes and pieces of baked bell pepper make the salad juicy and saturate it with bright summer colors. And thanks to the fried pieces of chicken fillet, the salad turns out to be filling. An incredibly bright, appetizing and piquant salad!


Ingredients (for 6 servings)
Chicken fillet - 400 g
Bell pepper - 1 pc.
Tomatoes - 300 g
Lettuce leaves - 1 bunch
Sesame seeds - 1 tbsp.
Soy sauce - 50 ml
Olive oil - 3 tbsp.
Balsamic vinegar - 1 tbsp.
Fresh dill - 2-3 sprigs
Salt - 1/3 tsp.
Ground black pepper - on the tip of a knife
