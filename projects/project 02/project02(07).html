Mushroom and Chicken Salad

Almost always, this salad with chicken, mushrooms, eggs and cheese "disappears" first from the table. If desired, you can add walnuts to the salad.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)
Chicken breast - 1 pc.
Chicken egg - 2 pcs.
Hard cheese - 100 g
Porcini mushrooms (fresh) - 150 g
Onions - 2 pcs.
Sunflower oil - 20 g
Mayonnaise
Fine salt
